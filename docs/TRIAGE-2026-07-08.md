## Systemic bug classes (one fix, many plugins)

1. **Dead MOD routes — per-sample smoother overwrite.** Modulated values written into
   `Settings`/`base` then overwritten by unmodulated `smoothed.next()` in the sample
   loop: GRIT (all 4 targets), TRACER (all 3), CARVE (mix), HALT (mix+out).
   IMPACT's retrofit is the correct reference pattern.
2. **Dry-path hard clamp at ±0.999.** Suite-wide `clamp(-0.999, 0.999)` on the final
   mixed output digitally clips >0 dBFS float headroom (routine on FL buses) even at
   mix=0, breaking every "mix=0 nulls" contract on hot signals: GRIT, TRACER, DRIFT,
   FLYBY, CHAMBER, BANDAID, PATINA, CHORALE, SWARM (+ others sharing the template).
   POLICY FIX: final clamp becomes ±8.0 (pure runaway/NaN guard, transparent for any
   real level); fuzz tests assert finite and <= +18 dBFS instead of <= 0 dBFS.
3. **configure() clobbers note-on state.** Key-tracked pitch overwritten at the next
   block boundary: IMPACT (f_end), SNAP (f_start/f_end), PLUCK (Vel->Bright damping).
   Fix: configure must not overwrite live per-note overrides (guard or reapply).
4. **ShiftEngine phase accumulators never wrap** (suite-core/src/shift.rs:265-266):
   SEANCE/VOXKEY/VOXFIT wet paths degrade over ~10+ min sessions. Fix: wrap mod 2pi.
5. **Freeze-mix blend branches on unsmoothed bool** -> click on toggle: EMBER
   (release), OUROBOROS, SWARM, MURMUR. Fix: one-pole the freeze blend factor.
6. **Bus instance-id collision** (suite-core/src/bus.rs new_instance_id): per-DLL
   counter means first instance of every plugin shares an id in un-bridged FL ->
   NERVE routes can bind to the wrong plugin; X-RAY legend/solo collide. Fix:
   allocate ids from a bus-file-global atomic counter. Plus: bounded seqlock reader
   spin (livelock guard) and MOD picker filtered to mod-publishing slots.
7. **"Sync" = rate-only, no transport phase-lock**: SEANCE (BROKEN — chop grid),
   DRIFT, SWARM, FLYBY, SHAPESHIFT orbit. Fix where it matters most (SEANCE now;
   others P2 backlog).
8. **GUI preset apply omissions**: EMBER + OUROBOROS skip freeze_mix.
