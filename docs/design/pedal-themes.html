Batch 3 — CONSOLE, developed

Theme 7 pushed to a real object: Hammond-style enclosure with machined depth, recessed screen bay with mounting screws, curved-glass amber CRT (scanlines and glare live only inside the glass), tick-ringed machined knobs, jack furniture, and a proper footswitch with a hex collar.
